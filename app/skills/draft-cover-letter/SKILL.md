# Draft cover letter

> Swimlane B — short **motivation letter** aligned to one job or company, grounded in CV facts.

## Inputs

- CV summary or structured fields.
- Job title, company name, and 2–3 requirements from the posting (trusted text only).

## Behavior

1. Opening: role + company + one concrete hook from CV or posting (no generic “I am writing…”).
2. Body: 2 short paragraphs — **fit** (skills ↔ requirements) and **motivation** (fact-based).
3. Closing: single call-to-action; polite, confident, not presumptuous.
4. Length: ~180–220 words unless user specifies otherwise.
5. Tone: professional, inclusive; avoid clichés and buzzword stuffing.

## Forbidden

- Claims not supported by CV or provided posting text.
- Salary demands, legal threats, or PII beyond what the user supplied.
